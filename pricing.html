Questions

Is the data real?
Yes. All lender behavior data is derived from CFPB HMDA public use files, 2018–2025. 2.9 million FHA records, 11 major lenders, 51 jurisdictions. No estimates, no surveys.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account dashboard at any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
Does this replace my mortgage broker?
No. FRC is intelligence, not advice. It tells you which lender types historically approve profiles like yours, based on 8 years of federal data. Your broker or lender makes the final decision.
What is the Broker Suite for?
Licensed mortgage brokers and real estate agents who want to run routing analysis for multiple clients, share branded PDF reports, and track client profiles in one dashboard.
Is there a free trial?
Free tier is permanent — no time limit, no credit card. Upgrade only when you need more routing decisions or the full lender matrix.
🔒
No SSN. No credit pull. No risk.

FRC never requests your Social Security Number or pulls your credit. All analysis is based on your self-reported profile matched against federal HMDA data patterns. Educational only — not financial advice.
