Buying a Home in Virginia Beach, VA?

The median home price in Virginia Beach, VA is approximately $365,000. With a 20% down payment at current rates (~6.7%), your principal and interest payment on a 30-year mortgage would be around $1,884/month — before taxes, insurance, and any HOA fees.

Virginia property taxes average 0.99% annually, which adds approximately $301/month on a $365,000 home. Always budget for the full PITI payment, not just the base mortgage.
