How to Improve Your Score Fast

30-day wins: Pay credit card balances to below 10% utilization. This single change can add 20-40 points.

60-90 day wins: Dispute errors on your credit report. 1 in 3 reports has a verifiable error. Each removed negative item can add 10-30 points.

6-month wins: Become an authorized user on a family member's old, clean account. Their history becomes yours.
