Frequently Asked Questions

Should I refinance if I have a 6.0% mortgage rate?
Probably not — if you locked in 6.0% or below, you already have a great rate. Current market rates are around 6.3-6.5%, so refinancing would actually increase your payment.
What are current mortgage refinance rates in 2026?
Current 30-year fixed refinance rates average around 6.3-6.7% as of May 2026, based on Freddie Mac data. Your actual rate will depend on your credit score, LTV ratio, and lender.
How long does it take to break even on a refinance?
Closing costs typically run 2-3% of the loan amount ($8,000-12,000 on a $400K loan). Divide closing costs by monthly savings to find your break-even in months. If you plan to stay in the home longer than that, refinancing makes sense.
