Why This Matters

Most loan officers can quote FHA guidelines. FRC Certified analysts go further — they can identify overlay-specific denials, explain why the same borrower gets different outcomes at different lenders, and route clients to the right program before they apply.

In a market where a 640 credit score gets denied at one bank and approved at another, this knowledge is the difference between losing a client and closing the loan.
