The Math

At today's 6.7% mortgage rate with 20% down, the monthly PITI on San Antonio's median home is $2,017. To keep housing under 36% of gross income (the back-end DTI threshold most lenders use), you need $85,000/year — 155% of the city's median income.

The local median income of $55,000 leaves a gap of $30,000/year. This means the median household needs to earn 55% more to comfortably qualify for the median home.
