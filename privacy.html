Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

What We Collect

FinanceRateCalc is designed to work without collecting personal identifying information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect Social Security numbers, dates of birth, or government ID numbers.
  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • Calculator inputs (income, debts, home price) are processed in your browser and are not stored on our servers.
  • If you voluntarily submit a contact form or partner application, we collect the information you provide (name, email, message).

Cookies & Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand aggregate traffic patterns — pages visited, general location, device type. This data is anonymized and used only to improve the platform. You can opt out via your browser settings or Google's opt-out tools.

Google AdSense

This site displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at google.com/settings/ads.

Third-Party Forms

Contact and partner application forms are processed by Formspree (formspree.io). Please review their privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Data Retention

Form submissions are retained for up to 12 months for business purposes. Anonymous denial data submissions (overlay leaderboard) are retained indefinitely in aggregate form only — no individual records are kept.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personally identifiable information you've submitted by emailing press@financeratecalc.com.
