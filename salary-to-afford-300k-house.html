⚠ Common Mistake

Most buyers drain their savings for the down payment and have nothing left for emergencies. Your furnace will break. Your roof will leak. Murphy's Law loves new homeowners. Always maintain a 6-month emergency fund after closing.

What Reduces the Required Salary?

  • Larger down payment: 30% down instead of 20% reduces your monthly payment by $-200/month
  • Better credit score: 760+ score can get you a rate 0.5-0.75% lower, saving $119/month
  • Lower DTI: Paying off car loans or student debt before applying increases buying power
  • ARM vs Fixed: A 5/1 ARM starts lower, but carries rate risk after year 5

Is a $300K Home Right for You?

The honest answer depends on three things: your income stability, your other debts, and your local market. A $300K home in Austin, TX is a median home. The same price in Detroit buys a mansion. Context matters enormously.
