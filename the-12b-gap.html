Why this keeps happening

The Market Blindness Index for FHA lending is 0.883 — on a scale where 1.0 means the market is completely blind to lender quality. Low-denial lenders do not gain market share. High-denial lenders do not lose it. The feedback loop that should punish Shadow Approvals doesn't exist.

Borrowers are assigned to lenders by advertising, broker relationships, and geographic accident — not by data. The information that would enable rational routing has existed in public federal records since 2018. It just hasn't been translated into a tool that works at the moment of decision.
