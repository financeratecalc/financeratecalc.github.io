What this is not

It is not a claim that any lender acted improperly — overlays are lawful business decisions. It is not an approval predictor for an individual: denial rates reflect who applies as well as how files are judged. And a warning for model builders: HMDA contains no credit scores, no employment-verification detail, and no application dates. Any system citing those from this dataset is fabricating.
