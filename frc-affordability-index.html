6. Methodology & Data Sources

The FRC Affordability Index draws from the following sources:

Denial patterns: Anonymous submissions to the FRC Mortgage Rejection Database (n=247), supplemented by HMDA public data and Federal Reserve consumer finance reports.

Home prices: NAR median existing home sale prices, Zillow Research, and local MLS data where available.

Income data: US Census Bureau American Community Survey, BLS Current Population Survey.

Mortgage rates: Freddie Mac Primary Mortgage Market Survey (30-year fixed, April 2026).

Lender denial rates: FRC Bank Rejection Engine usage data and HMDA loan-level data. National bank and credit union rates are approximate based on public filings.
