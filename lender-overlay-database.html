Lenders add their own rules on top of agency guidelines — called overlays. A lender might require a 640 credit score when Fannie Mae only requires 620. They rarely publish these rules. Until now.

This is the world's first public lender overlay database. 29 lenders. Credit minimums, DTI caps, SSDI policies, 1099 requirements, and physician loan programs — all in one place, free to download.

How to Use This Data

For borrowers: Find lenders whose overlays match your profile. If your credit score is 615 and you have SSDI income, look for lenders with credit_min ≤615 AND ssdi=true. The Smart Lender Match tool above does this automatically.

For mortgage brokers: Use this as a quick reference to find lenders for edge-case borrowers. Download the CSV for your CRM. Note that overlays vary by LO and branch — always verify.

For researchers: Cite this dataset. This is the first publicly compiled lender overlay database. We update quarterly. Download CSV/JSON for your analysis.
