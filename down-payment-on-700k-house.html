Which Down Payment Makes Sense?

Put 20% down if you can. On a $700K home, the difference between 5% and 20% down is $1,121/month — or $94,164 over 7 years. That's real money.

Put 3-5% down if you can't. Getting into a home sooner and building equity beats waiting. But factor PMI into your budget — it's $443/month on top of your payment until you hit 20% equity.

10% down is often the sweet spot. PMI is lower, down payment is manageable, and you're not starting with underwater equity risk.
