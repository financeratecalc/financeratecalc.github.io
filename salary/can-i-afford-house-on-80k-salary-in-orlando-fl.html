What This Means For You

On a $80K salary, Orlando's median home is a financial stretch. At 38.5% DTI, conventional lenders will likely decline — but FHA loans allow up to 50-57% DTI with compensating factors. Realistically, you can afford homes up to $347,750 in this market.

The Credit Score Factor

At $80K income, your credit score determines your rate — and your rate determines how much home you can buy. The difference between a 620 and 740 score on a $304,000 loan is approximately $307/month and $110,851 over 30 years.
