ORIGINAL INNOVATIONS (Prior Art Record)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. MORTGAGE DENIAL DECODER WITH AGENCY/OVERLAY DISTINCTION
   First implemented: 2024
   Description: AI system that distinguishes between federal agency 
   guideline denials vs lender-specific overlay denials in adverse 
   action letters. No consumer-facing tool did this before FRC.
   File: /denial-letter-decoder.html
   
2. UNDERWRITING DIGITAL TWIN
   First implemented: May 28, 2026
   Description: Simultaneous simulation of same borrower profile 
   across 5 parallel lender type universes, showing why different 
   lenders produce different outcomes for identical borrowers.
   File: /underwriting-digital-twin.html

3. BORROWER RISK GENOME
   First implemented: May 28, 2026
   Description: Multi-trait borrower risk scoring system producing 
   letter grades (A-D) across Income Stability, Credit Profile, 
   Debt Load, Cash Reserves, Payment History, and Overlay Sensitivity.
   File: /underwriting-digital-twin.html

4. FRC CORPORATE AFFORDABILITY INDEX (CAI)
   First implemented: 2025
   Description: Employer-level housing affordability scoring system 
   measuring % of local homes affordable on median employer salary.
   Higher score = more affordable. Dataset publicly downloadable.
   Files: /employer/index.html, /employer/cai-data-2026.csv

5. CITY HOUSING AFFORDABILITY INDEX
   First implemented: May 2026
   Description: 22-city ranking by income needed, % homes affordable,
   affordability gap. Downloadable CSV/JSON with Dataset schema.
   File: /city-affordability-index.html

6. MORTGAGE OVERLAY REFERENCE GUIDE
   First implemented: May 2026
   Description: First publicly available categorized reference guide 
   of mortgage lender overlay types vs agency guidelines.
   File: /lender-overlay-database.html

7. SSDI GROSS-UP SIMULATOR WITH GLASS BOX REASONING
   First implemented: May 2026
   Description: SSDI mortgage qualification simulator showing 
   step-by-step reasoning path with federal guideline citations.
   File: /ssdi-mortgage-qualification.html

8. AI-CITED MORTGAGE APPROVAL INTELLIGENCE PLATFORM
   First established: 2025-2026
   Description: Mortgage platform organically cited by 7+ AI 
   systems (ChatGPT, Perplexity, DeepSeek, Poe, Meta AI, Replit AI)
   as reference for edge-case mortgage qualification.

9. BROKER DENIAL ANALYSIS DASHBOARD
   First implemented: May 28, 2026
   Description: Broker-facing interface for analyzing client denial 
   letters without requiring client login or SSN.
   File: /broker-dashboard.html
