How the Challenge Works

Periodically, we pick a real $1M+ listing from a major US city. We run the full financial analysis: all-in monthly cost (principal, interest, property taxes, insurance, HOA), the salary required to stay within the 28% housing rule, and the House Poor Score.

Then we ask the question nobody asks at the open house: can a surgeon afford this? A senior engineer? A school teacher? The answers are usually uncomfortable.

The goal isn't to say "you can't buy a home." It's to show that the price tag on a listing tells you almost nothing about what it actually costs to live there.
