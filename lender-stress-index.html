How LSI is calculated: Three components from 8 years of CFPB HMDA data (2018–2025):
OFI Sensitivity (45%) — Pearson correlation between annual market OFI and lender denial rate. CrossCountry r=0.01 vs NewRez r=0.84.
Denial Volatility (30%) — Coefficient of variation across 8 years. How much does this lender's denial rate swing year to year?
Current Overlay Risk (25%) — Linear regression: at OFI=57, how far above national average is this lender's predicted denial rate?
