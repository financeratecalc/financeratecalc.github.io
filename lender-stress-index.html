What LSI measures: How much does this lender's FHA denial rate rise when market OFI increases? Based on 8 years × 11 lenders × 51 states of CFPB HMDA data. LSI is not a quality score — a lender with LSI 6 may still have a higher denial rate than one with LSI 57. It measures sensitivity to market conditions, not absolute performance.
