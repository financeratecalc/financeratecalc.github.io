IV. Regulatory Context: Your Adverse-Action Rights

Under the Equal Credit Opportunity Act (Reg B) you are entitled to a written statement of specific denial reasons within 30 days of a completed application. Under FCRA §615, if a credit report influenced the decision, you are entitled to the name of the bureau used and a free copy of that report. These are federal rights — exercising them is routine, not adversarial. This section is educational; it is not legal advice, and this document makes no finding that any lender acted improperly. Overlays are lawful business decisions.
