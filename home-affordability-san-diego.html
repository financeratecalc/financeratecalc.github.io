Frequently Asked Questions

What salary do I need to buy a home in San Diego?
Based on the median San Diego home price of $895,000 and standard 28% front-end DTI ratio, you need a gross income of at least $190,000/year to qualify for a conventional mortgage. With existing debt obligations (car payments, student loans), you may need more.
Is San Diego a buyer's or seller's market in 2026?
Based on FRC Buyer Demand data from active listings, San Diego is currently showing COOLING demand. This is a transitioning market — some negotiating room exists but well-priced homes still move quickly.
What is the down payment for a home in San Diego?
For the median San Diego home ($895,000): 20% conventional down payment = $179,000. FHA minimum 3.5% down = $31,325. FHA requires a credit score of 580+. Conventional loans typically require 620+.
