FHA denial rates by metro area, 2025.
The only free FHA-specific metro table.

Computed from the complete 2025 federal HMDA record: every FHA application reaching a credit decision, aggregated to 320 metropolitan areas and divisions. Among the 215 metros with at least 1,000 decisioned applications, the denial rate ran from 8.9% in Amarillo, TX to 31.8% in Bridgeport-Stamford-Norwalk, CT — a 3.6× spread by geography alone, before a single lender is chosen.

The metro small-loan penalty: the loan-size gap seen at the state level sharpens at metro resolution. In El Paso, TX, a small FHA application (<$150K) was denied 3.61× as often as a large one (32.1% vs 8.9%) in 2025 — the widest measured metro penalty. Existing metro-level mortgage studies track approvals or demographics; none publishes the FHA loan-size axis. This table does.
