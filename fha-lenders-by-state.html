The FHA door map: lender denial rates in America’s 15 biggest markets

Direct answer · updated 2026-07-19

The soft door travels with the lender, not the state. CrossCountry Mortgage posted among the lowest observed FHA denial rates in every major market in 2025 — 4.3% in Texas, 4.3% in California, 6.8% in Florida — while the hardest doors (AmeriSave, Lakeview) ran 55–82% in the same states. Below: every high-volume lender’s observed denial rate in the 15 largest FHA markets, from the complete 2025 federal record (cells with 100+ decisioned applications).
