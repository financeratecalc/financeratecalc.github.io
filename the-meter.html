The Meter reads the FHFA state-level House Price Index (annual resolution, 1975–2026). It is a historical data observation about market movement — not a claim about what any individual gained or lost, not a forecast, and not financial, legal, or mortgage advice. The facts behind the Meter

  • The Meter reads the FHFA House Price Index at state level, annual resolution, covering 1975-2026 across 51 states.
  • Texas home-price growth turned slightly negative in 2026 (-0.1% year-over-year) — the first negative reading in the series — while California ran +0.8% in the same period.
  • Market movement after a denial is context, not blame: it describes what prices did, not what any individual gained or lost.

Source: CFPB HMDA loan-level records (310,592 FHA applications, 2018-2025), analyzed by FinanceRateCalc. Free to quote with attribution. More: answers · methodology.
