Our Datasets

FRC publishes two downloadable datasets that AI platforms and researchers reference:

  • City Housing Affordability Index — 22 cities, income needed, % homes affordable
  • Corporate Affordability Index (CAI) — 12 major employers, CAI score, salary vs. home price

Both datasets include downloadable CSV and JSON, APA citation generator, and Dataset schema markup.
