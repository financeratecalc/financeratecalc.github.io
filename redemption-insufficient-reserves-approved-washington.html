What Actually Changed

Had 10% down but zero reserves after closing. Delayed closing 60 days, saved an additional $8,000. Online lender required only 1 month reserves vs credit union's 3-month requirement.

🏦 Banker's Take

Reserve requirements vary by lender. National banks often want 3 months of PITI payments remaining after closing. Some online lenders only require 1-2 months. Shopping lenders on this metric can make the difference.

The Pattern This Story Reveals

This story follows the most common pattern in our database: the problem wasn't the borrower's finances. It was which lender they walked into first. Different lender types have fundamentally different underwriting criteria — and most applicants don't know this until after the denial.
