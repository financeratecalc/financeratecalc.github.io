What Salary Do You Need to Own in Brooklyn Heights?

Using the standard 28% front-end housing ratio, you need $365,124/year in gross income to comfortably afford the median home in Brooklyn Heights at current rates. That's the salary required just for housing — before food, transportation, retirement savings, or healthcare.

The median household income in New York is approximately $200,818/year. That means the typical New York household is $164,306 short of what's needed to buy in Brooklyn Heights.
