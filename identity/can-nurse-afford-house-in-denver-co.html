🏥 Nurse-Specific Mortgage Facts

Income note: RN base + overtime + shift differentials. 2-year history required for overtime to count.

Key challenge: Travel nurses face extra scrutiny. Agency income requires 2 years of consistent 1099 history.

Your advantage: FHA loans work well for nurses — up to 50-57% DTI with compensating factors. Some states have healthcare worker housing programs.

The Real Math

On a $85,000 annual salary, your gross monthly income is $7,083. The safe housing threshold (28% front-end DTI) gives you a max monthly payment of $1,983. Denver's median home payment is $3,379/month — leaving you $829/month over budget.

Renting in Denver costs roughly $1,820/month for a 1BR. Buying at 36% DTI would require your maximum affordable price of $399,052 — below the city median.
