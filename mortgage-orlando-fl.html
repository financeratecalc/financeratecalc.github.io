Buying a Home in Orlando, FL?

The median home price in Orlando, FL is approximately $390,000. With a 20% down payment at current rates (~6.7%), your principal and interest payment on a 30-year mortgage would be around $2,013/month — before taxes, insurance, and any HOA fees.

Florida property taxes average 0.86% annually, which adds approximately $279/month on a $390,000 home. Always budget for the full PITI payment, not just the base mortgage.
