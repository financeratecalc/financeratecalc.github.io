Guild FHA Denial Rate (2025): the behavioral file

Direct answer · updated 2026-07-16

Guild denied 8.6% of its FHA applications in 2025 — rank #2 of 11 major lenders (1 = lowest denial), from CFPB HMDA loan-level records (310,592 FHA applications, 2018-2025).

Sharpest observed DTI step: at CLTV 90-94, denial moves from 22.6% at DTI 44 to 39.1% at DTI 45 — a +16.5 point jump across one integer of DTI. FHA program rules permit DTI well above this; the desk does not.

Sharpest observed CLTV step: at DTI 38, denial moves from 32.5% in the 90-94% band to 80.4% in the 97+% band — +47.9 points on down payment alone.
