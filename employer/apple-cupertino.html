The Reality

Apple's Cupertino campus sits in one of Silicon Valley's most expensive markets. At $168,000 median salary, employees face a housing cost that would require 79% of income — making homeownership near campus essentially impossible for most.

Apple's architectural showcase campus, Apple Park, sits in Cupertino where the median home exceeds $1.85M. The majority of Apple's non-executive workforce commutes from San Jose, the East Bay, or increasingly from Santa Cruz and Gilroy — adding 30-60 minutes to each commute. The company has discussed employee housing partnerships but no large-scale solution has materialized.
