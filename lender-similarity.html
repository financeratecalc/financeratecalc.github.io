Methodology: Each lender is represented as a 15-dimensional feature vector: 2025 denial rate, 2024 denial rate, year-over-year trend, volatility, LSI score, overlay risk, OFI sensitivity, CLTV cliff (+pp at 85% LTV), DTI cliff (+pp at 44-45% DTI), mid-risk disparity, high-risk disparity, OFI score, refi volume ratio, purchase sensitivity, and cash instability. Vectors are min-max normalized and compared using cosine similarity. Score of 1.0 = identical behavior, 0.0 = completely orthogonal. Source: CFPB HMDA 2025, FRC internal analysis.
