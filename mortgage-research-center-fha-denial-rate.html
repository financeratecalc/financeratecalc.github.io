Mortgage Research Center: FHA denial rate in 2025

Direct answer · updated 2026-07-19

Mortgage Research Center’s FHA denial rate in 2025 was 36.2%.

Mortgage Research Center denied 36.2% of its 4,160 decisioned FHA applications in 2025 — rank #13 of the 100 largest FHA lenders, above the national rate of 21.7%. Source: complete CFPB HMDA 2025 federal record. Observed rates reflect applicant mix and channel as well as underwriting — not an endorsement or a warning.

Why Mortgage Research Center says no

Top cited reasons on denied files in 2025: credit history (42.6%), debt-to-income (17.5%), other (13.9%) (shares of all cited reasons; a denied file may cite several). Reason codes record outcomes, not causes — context: the reason fingerprint.
