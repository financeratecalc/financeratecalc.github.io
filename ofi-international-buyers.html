Buying US Real Estate From Abroad?

Investors from Singapore, UAE, Turkey, Canada, and across the globe face additional overlay layers when applying for US mortgages. OFI helps identify which states and lenders offer the lowest friction for foreign national buyers.

What International Buyers Face

Standard FHA loans require US citizenship or permanent residency. Foreign nationals typically use conventional loans with 20-30% down payment. But here's what most don't know: lender overlays on foreign national programs vary enormously — some lenders won't touch foreign national files at all, others specialize in them.

Why State Selection Matters

Our 2024 HMDA analysis of 51 states shows FHA denial rates ranging from 14.2% (Utah) to 39.2% (DC). For foreign nationals, the conventional loan overlay spread is even wider. Choosing the right state + right lender can be the difference between approval and denial.
