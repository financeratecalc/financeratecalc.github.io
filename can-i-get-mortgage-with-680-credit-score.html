Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum credit score for a mortgage?
FHA loans accept scores as low as 580 (with 10% down) or 500 (with 10% down). Conventional loans typically require 620+. VA and USDA loans have no official minimum but most lenders require 620+.
How long does it take to improve a 680 credit score?
Most people see meaningful improvement in 60-90 days with focused action. Significant improvements (40+ points) typically take 6-12 months. The fastest wins come from reducing credit utilization and disputing errors.
Should I wait to buy a house until my credit improves?
It depends on your market and timeline. A 60-point improvement could save $40,000+ over 30 years — often worth 3-6 months of waiting. But if home prices are rising faster than your interest savings, buying sooner may make more sense. Run the numbers for your specific situation.
