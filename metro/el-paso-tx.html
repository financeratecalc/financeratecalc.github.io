El Paso, TX: 10.8% of FHA applications were denied in 2025.

Of 3,798 FHA applications reaching a credit decision in this metro in 2025, 411 were formally denied. Small applications (<$150K) were denied at 32.1% vs 8.9% for large ones ($250K+) — a 3.61× penalty.
