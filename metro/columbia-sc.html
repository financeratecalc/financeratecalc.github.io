Columbia, SC: 20.6% of FHA applications were denied in 2025.

Of 5,384 FHA applications reaching a credit decision in this metro in 2025, 1,111 were formally denied. Small applications (<$150K) were denied at 38.9% vs 18.2% for large ones ($250K+) — a 2.14× penalty.
