2. What Are Affiliate Links?

An affiliate link is a specially tracked URL. When you click on an affiliate link on our site and complete an action (such as applying for a loan, opening an account, or making a purchase), the affiliate partner can identify that you came from our website. We then receive a commission for referring you.

This commission comes from the affiliate partner — not from you. You pay nothing extra by using our affiliate links.
