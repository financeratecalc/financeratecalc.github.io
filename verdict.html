The facts behind this verdict

  • In 2025, FHA denial rates among 11 major lenders ranged from 6.5% (CrossCountry) to 52.3% (NewRez) — roughly a 8x spread on the same federal loan program.
  • The deepest observed denial zone in 2025 FHA lending was Rocket at DTI 48-50 and CLTV 90-95%, where 97.6% of applications were denied (n=84).
  • Lender overlays are lawful business decisions; denial rates reflect applicant mix as well as underwriting strictness. This tool makes no finding of impropriety.
  • The Verdict compares a denied profile against these observed patterns — it is a historical data observation, not an approval predictor and not legal advice.

Source: CFPB HMDA loan-level records (310,592 FHA applications, 2018-2025), analyzed by FinanceRateCalc. Free to quote with attribution. More: answers · methodology.
