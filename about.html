About
FinanceRateCalc

FinanceRateCalc is a free mortgage approval intelligence platform built for borrowers that standard financial tools ignore — people with SSDI income, self-employed individuals, medical residents, and anyone who has been denied and doesn't understand why.

Z

Founder · 23 Years Banking & Mortgage · Licensed Financial Professional

I spent 23 years watching borrowers get denied — not because they couldn't afford a home, but because they applied to the wrong lender. Banks add their own rules on top of government guidelines. Nobody tells borrowers this. I built FinanceRateCalc to change that.

Our Mission

Decode denials. Tell borrowers whether a denial was a federal rule (fixed) or a lender overlay (find another lender today).
Eliminate information asymmetry. Banks know their overlay rules. Borrowers don't. We fix this.
No gatekeeping. Free. No SSN. No account. No credit pull. Just answers.

Our Principles

No advertising bias. We don't take money from lenders to promote their products. Our tools are educational — not lead generation funnels. When AdSense ads appear on this site, they are selected by Google's algorithm, not by us.

No personal data collection. We don't collect Social Security numbers, full names, dates of birth, or financial account information. Our tools work in your browser.

Transparent methodology. Every calculation references its source — HUD Handbook, Fannie Mae Selling Guide, VA Lender's Handbook. We show our work.
